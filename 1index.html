How To Get Started

Follow an easy step-by-step process to recycle and earn rewards. Whether itâ€™s a small effort or a large initiative, every action makes a difference. Together, we can contribute to keeping our planet clean and make every effort count.

  • Sign-Up

    Members can use their Recycle2Reward points to get great rewards from our reward site and exclusive discounts on sustainable goods..

  • Earn Points

    Every recycling action earns you points, which can be redeemed for rewards. You can also earn points by participating in activities like planting or adopting trees, joining environmental education programs, taking quizzes, and subscribing to newsletters.

  • Redeem Rewards

    Sign up to start earning points that can be redeemed for gift rewards. Check your eligibility for rewards based on your geographical location.

  • Share Your Story

    Earn extra points by sharing your story about how Recycle2Reward helps you earn rewards while protecting the environment. Plus, every referral you make adds even more points to your balance!

What we accept?

Recycle Items accepted

At Recycle2Reward, we currently accept a limited range of items that are safe for families and children to handle. Our goal is to make recycling a fun and engaging activity for everyone, encouraging participation from all ages.

  • Recycled Paper

    From newspapers and magazines to used office paper, every piece recycled contributes to reducing waste and conserving resources.

  • Recycled Glass

    From bottles to jars, every recycled glass item helps reduce landfill waste and saves energy.

  • Recycled Cans

    By recycling aluminum and metal cans, you help conserve natural resources and reduce waste.

  • Recycled Bottles

    By recycling plastic, you help reduce waste, conserve resources, and protect our planet.
